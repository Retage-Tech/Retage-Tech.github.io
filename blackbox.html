The Mystery Black Box is a box that has an unknown mechanism. The unknown mechanism allows water through it from time to time yet, when water is passed through it (Trial 1: 0ml) isn't the same amount that entered (Trial 1: 1000 ml). So, we had to design what we believed the mechanism was.

In the original version we conducted experiments whose data was inconsistent.This didn't help provide any more evidence to help design the unknown mechanism. Since, the data was inconsistent from Mr. Sinusas's black box did help rule out previous concepts for the mechanism. Although the data was contradictory with it having trial 4's input being 1000ml the output would be 370ml even though both trial 1and 2 had no output (trial 3 N/A), it allowed ideas of mechanisms that had two places where water could be stored to arise. With the new concepts of what the unknown mechanism could be, my team and I chose what we believed was most accurate.

In the version my team made, we had two containers on differing sides. We had three tubes total, the first was connected to a funnel at the top of the box; feeding into the first container that was held on the far left. The second tube was connected to the bottom of the first container and the top of the second container as the second container was placed slightly below the first container to allow gravity to pull the water down the tube. While the third tube was placed near the mid-top of the second container and out through a hole in the box where the water can escape to mimic the original black box where a tube is leading out of it allowing water to escape.
