Screenprinting Project Final Webpage

By: Retage Soliman

DNA Experiment Blackbox Investigation Homepage

This screenprinting project is an artpeice made to induce philosophical thinking and reflection on reality.This project was heavily inspired by the state of the world and how people in power are trying to confuse us.

Steps

  1. Plan out Screenprint Designs
  2. Finalize Designs
  3. Use Length of Design and Measure Wood for Frame
  4. Cut out Wood
  5. Glue and Staple Wood Together
  6. Cut and Staple Mesh to Frame
  7. Cut and Print out Design on Vinyl
  8. Weed out Blank Spaces of the vinyl
  9. Iron on Design Vinyl to Mesh
  10. Get Ink of choice and material of choice
  11. Print out design

Goal/Impact

I intended for this project to impact society in a way that's meaningful. I want my art to be about how people see issues with the system they live in and how they should be trying to change it.
